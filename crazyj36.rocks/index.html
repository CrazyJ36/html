Biography

An Independent Instrumental Guitarist, CrazyJ36s' Guitar Music is Original, and Exciting! The Music is meant to please the casual listener. Targeting A totally unique genre 'New Age Rock'. The Guitarist, Songwriter, and Producer behind the name is Thomas James Kennedy, born 1989 in Tampa, Florida, now residing in Central Florida. A self-taught Musician, CrazyJ36 decoded what Music Theory is on his own with one Musical Keyboard, and has been making Music since his childhood, always looking to hear the coolest sounds possible. In his personal time, for self-healing, he practices A mix between Tai-Chi and Shamanism, practices which also blend into his Musics' feel. Also A software developer, you can check out his programming code below in the 'Interact and Learn' section. Lone thoughts, inspiration, and important news below that in the 'blog' section.
